The Turkish bath (hammam) is one of the most complex stone environments in luxury architecture. High humidity, temperature swings, and demands for safe, beautiful surfaces make stone selection and production highly specialized. Eskan Stone has practical experience through the [UAE Turkish Bath](/en/special-works/turkish-bath-uae) production and export project.

## Turkish Bath Space Characteristics

A hammam includes:

- **Frigidarium:** Cool entry space
- **Caldarium:** Steam room with heated floor and walls
- **Domed ceiling (Kümbet):** Dome with light oculus
- **Stone paving and platforms:** Seating and treatment surfaces

Each zone has different stone requirements.

## Stone Selection for Hammams

### Marble

White marble (cream or Carrara-type) is the traditional hammam symbol.

**Advantages:** Beauty, polishability, luxury feel  
**Notes:** Requires sealing; polished floors are slippery when wet — use brushed or sandblasted finish

### Travertine

For walls and decorative elements. Natural porosity absorbs moisture — must be filled and sealed.

### Acid-Resistant Stone

In steam environments, condensate can be acidic. High acid-resistance stone (some granites) suits heated floor zones better.

## Produced Stone Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Caldarium flooring | Thick stone over underfloor heating |
| Central platform (Göbektaşı) | Round or octagonal treatment stone |
| Wall cladding | Marble panels with minimal joints |
| Dome | Curved stone segments — CNC production |
| Edges and drains | Sloped for water runoff |

## CNC Production for Hammams

Domed ceiling segments and steps are produced on **5-axis CNC**. Cut precision is essential for tight assembly — moisture penetrates open joints.

In the UAE project:
- White marble and cream travertine
- 150+ numbered pieces
- Export packaging per [export quality standards](/en/blog/export-quality-standards)

## Installation and Waterproofing

Critical installation points:

1. **Polyurethane or heat-resistant epoxy** joints
2. **Floor slope** toward drains
3. **Expansion isolation** around central platform
4. **Behind-panel waterproofing** on wet walls

## Exporting Stone Hammams

Demand for luxury Turkish baths in the UAE, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia is growing. Production in Iran offers competitive cost with quality comparable to Europe.

Every stone piece installed on a villa facade, Turkish bath, or luxury showroom travels a long path from quarry to factory to site. This article walks through production stages at the [Eskan Stone factory](/en/capabilities) in Yazd — the same process projects like [Villa Mollasadra](/en/projects/villa-mollasadra) and the [UAE Turkish Bath](/en/special-works/turkish-bath-uae) underwent.

## Stage 1: Block Selection and Procurement

Production begins with selecting raw stone blocks from travertine and marble quarries in central Iran.

**Selection criteria:**
- Color and texture matching client-approved sample
- No structural cracks or fractures
- Sufficient dimensions for piece nesting optimization

Blocks are crane-loaded and transported to the Yazd factory.

## Stage 2: Primary Cutting (Gang Saw / Wire Saw)

Blocks are cut into slabs of required thickness. This stage determines how many pieces each block yields.

## Stage 3: Design and CNC Programming

After receiving architectural drawings:

1. 3D model each piece
2. Optimize nesting on slabs
3. Generate toolpaths for 5-axis machines
4. Simulate cuts to prevent tool collision

## Stage 4: CNC Cutting

Eskan Stone 5-axis CNC machines carve pieces with millimeter precision:

- Capitals and cornices
- Facade panels with anchor holes
- Fountain and pool elements
- Interior wall cladding

Operators monitor cutting and perform interim measurements.

## Stage 5: Surface Finishing

Depending on design:

| Finish | Application |
|--------|-------------|
| Honed | Matte modern facades |
| Polished | Interior marble, capitals |
| Brushed | Slip-resistant exteriors |
| Sandblasted | Granular texture, deep shadow |

## Stage 6: Quality Control

Each piece is inspected for:

- Dimensions (digital caliper)
- Surface finish
- Color match within project batch
- Visual defects (cracks, chipped edges)

Non-conforming pieces are labeled and segregated.

## Stage 7: Packaging

- Wooden crates with protective framing
- Foam between pieces
- Piece number and project labels
- For export: palletizing and shrink wrap

## Stage 8: Shipping and Installation

- Road transport to Tehran, Isfahan, and other cities
- Bandar Abbas shipping for Gulf export
- Shop drawings and packing list with shipment
- On-site technical support (by agreement)

## Factory Visits

Eskan Stone welcomes architects, clients, and contractors to tour the production line. During a visit you can:

- Watch CNC machines in operation
- Handle produced sample pieces
- Consult the technical team about your project

## Conclusion

Production transparency builds trust. From quarry to installation, every stage is quality-controlled.
